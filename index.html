Deck size

The number of symbols you upload equals the number of cards in your deck — e.g. 31 symbols makes 31 cards.

Cards: 31
Symbols per card: 6
Unique symbols needed: 31

Every two cards will share exactly one matching symbol — that's the math that makes Spot it! work.

🃏 Fun fact: the original Spot it! has 57 symbols but only 55 cards, allegedly to allow the cards to be printed on card-making machines designed for standard playing card decks (52 plus 2 jokers and 1 advertising card). (Wikipedia: Dobble)
